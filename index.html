About me

I am a PhD student at the Department of Computer Sciences, UW-Madison since January 2021. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Christos Tzamos. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics in Summer 2020 from Nanjing University. Before starting my PhD study, I was a visiting student at ITCS, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, where I was fortunate to be hosted by Prof. Nick Gravin, Prof. Zhihao Gavin Tang and Prof. Xiao Wang.

Research
