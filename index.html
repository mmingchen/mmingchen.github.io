About me

I am a PhD student at the Department of Computer Sciences, UW-Madison since 2021. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Christos Tzamos. I obtained my B.S. in Mathematics in Summer 2020 from the Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University. Before starting my PhD study, I spent a year as a visiting student at ITCS, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics.

Research
